(p ∧ q) → r <=> p → (q → r )
<pre>
(P ^ Q)' v R <=> P' v (Q' v R)      IMPLICATION x3
('P v 'Q) v R <=> P' v (Q' v R)     DEMORGAN'S LAW
('P v 'Q) v R <=> (P' v Q') v R     ASSOCIATIVE
</pre>
(q ∨ r ) → p <=> (q → p)∧(r → p)
<pre>
(Q v R)' v P <=> (Q' v P) ^ (R' v P)           IMPLICATION x3

(Q' v P) ^ (R' v P) <=> (Q' v P) ^ (R' v P)    DISTRIBUTIVE
</pre>
